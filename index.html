Our Story

Just a Small Family
Doing Big Flavors

We're Filé's Burgers — a small, family-owned burger joint right here in San Antonio, TX. We started simple: make awesome food, treat people right, and support the local community.

From our handcrafted burgers to our crispy tacos and loaded nachos, every item on our menu is made with care. We believe in real food, real flavor, and keeping it local.

Thank you for #EatingLocal and supporting small businesses like ours.
